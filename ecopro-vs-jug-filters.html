Being Honest

When a jug filter might still be right for you

The EcoPro isn't for everyone. Here's when we'd suggest sticking with a jug — or not buying a tap filter at all.

A jug filter might suit you better if…
💧
You only care about taste and chlorine reduction, and have no concerns about PFAS, fluoride, microplastics, or pharmaceuticals. Brita and Philips do a reasonable job on taste for less money per litre.
🚿
Your tap physically can't accept a filter — pull-out spray heads, square or angular aerator fittings, or non-standard thread sizes. Check our compatibility guide before purchasing.
🧊
You prefer cold filtered water from the fridge. The EcoPro filters at tap temperature — it doesn't refrigerate the output. A jug chilled in the fridge wins here.
🏠
You're renting and your landlord or body corporate has explicitly restricted tap modifications (rare but possible). The EcoPro is designed to be renter-friendly — no drilling — but check with your landlord if unsure.
