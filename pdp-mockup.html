Common questions
Will it fit my tap?
Adapters are included and cover 95% of Australian taps — kitchen mixers, gooseneck taps, basin mixers and more. Doesn't fit pull-out spray taps, square/angular taps, or sensor taps. If you're unsure, send us a photo and we'll confirm before you order — and send the right adapter free.
What's the difference between the Compact and Chrome SMR?
Same 5-stage filtration core. Same lab results. Same PFAS, chlorine, and microplastic removal. The Chrome SMR adds a 6th stage — Swedish Mineral Rock™ — that puts calcium, magnesium, and potassium back into your filtered water. If you want pure, clean water, the Compact is brilliant. If you want filtered and remineralised, get the SMR.
How often do I replace the cartridge?
Compact: every 2 months or 220L. SMR: every 3 months or 1,200L. Replace on schedule even if flow seems normal — an exhausted cartridge still flows but stops filtering. Annual Pack gives you all the refills upfront so you're never caught out.
Does it remove fluoride?
70% fluoride reduction — most carbon filters remove 0%. We publish the honest number. For >99% fluoride removal, see our RO Countertop.
I'm a renter — can I use this?
Yes — it's designed for renters. Screws onto your existing tap aerator in 60 seconds. No drilling, no plumber, no damage. Take it with you when you move.
